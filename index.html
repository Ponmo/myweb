Animal Fun Facts
Elephant
  1. It's the largest land animal in the world!
  2. They can communicate in a variety of ways. The most interesting is through vibrations in the ground
  3. An elephant has better memory than a human!
Octopus
  1. They have three hearts, one for the organs, and the other two for the gills!
  2. The heart that pumps blood to the organs cannot function when swimming
  3. Octopi are one of the most intelligent creatures, and some are able to use tools!
