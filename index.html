Elephant
  1. They are the largest land animal in the world!
  2. Elephants can communicate through vibrations
  3. An elephant has better memory than a human!
Octopus
  1. They have three hearts, one for the organs, and the other two for the gills
  2. The heart that pumps blood to the organs cannot function when swimming
  3. Octopuses are able to use tools

Octopuses do not have conventional hearing, and so cannot communicate with sound
